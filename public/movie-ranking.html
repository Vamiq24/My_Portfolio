The Best Movies According to Vamiq

My top 3 movies of all-time

3 Idiots

The College life is a phase in one's life which results not only a sheet of paper but the endless memories. This movie shows that phase in a very beautiful way with a strong message of "Let the child follow his/her passion and ambitions."

Chennai Express

It is the movie that every SRK fan admire off. It is one of the best comedy movie of Bollywood so far. The story revolves around a character who accidentally board the wrong train and reached a place which is totally different in culture and everything as that of his place. The Climax ends up with a social message of giving respect and importance to our daughters and consider thier lives equal to that of a son(giving them the right to chose for themselves the way of life they wanted to lead)

Dangal

Yet again an inspirational and one of the most successful Bollywood movies of all time is Dangal. It is a biopic of one of the best female wrestler of the country. The movie shows the struggle a girl from a stereotypic society has to face. The story revolves around the struggles and hard-work that she did with the guidance of his father who wanted to be a wrestler but was unable to pursue his dream due to family responsibilities.
